SECTION 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW
---

Acme Cloud is a cloud-based project management and collaboration platform designed for
teams of all sizes. We help businesses plan, track, and ship work faster using AI-assisted
workflows, real-time collaboration, and powerful reporting tools.

Founded in 2019, Acme Cloud serves over 15,000 businesses in 42 countries. Our platform is
available as a web application, iOS app, and Android app.

Core products:
- Acme Cloud Core: Task and project management
- Acme Cloud Reports: Advanced analytics and dashboards
- Acme Cloud Automations: Workflow automation engine
- Acme Cloud AI: AI-powered task suggestions and summaries (beta)

SECTION 3: FREE TRIAL
---

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start the trial.

During the trial:
- You get full access to all Business Plan features
- You can invite unlimited team members
- Your data is preserved if you upgrade after the trial ends
- You will receive reminder emails at 7 days and 1 day before expiry

To start a trial, visit acmecloud.com/trial and create an account.

If your trial expires without upgrading, your account moves to the Free plan automatically.
You will NOT lose your data — projects and tasks are archived and restored upon upgrade.

SECTION 4: BILLING AND PAYMENTS
---

Accepted payment methods:
- All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise plans only, invoiced quarterly)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise plans only)

Billing cycles:
- Monthly plans: Charged on the same date each month (the date you first subscribed)
- Annual plans: Charged once per year on your subscription anniversary

Invoices are emailed automatically to the billing email on your account. You can also
download past invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

If a payment fails:
- We retry the charge after 3 days, then again after 7 days
- Your account is not suspended until after the second failed retry
- You will receive email notifications for each failed attempt
- To update your payment method, go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods

Currency: All prices are listed and charged in USD. Currency conversion is handled by your
card issuer or PayPal.

SECTION 5: REFUND POLICY
---

MONTHLY PLANS
Monthly subscriptions are not eligible for refunds once the billing period has started.
However, if you cancel before your next billing date, you will retain access for the
remainder of your current billing period.

ANNUAL PLANS
Annual subscriptions are eligible for a pro-rated refund within 30 days of the annual
billing date. After 30 days, no refund is issued but you retain access until the end of
the annual period.

Example: If you paid $336/year ($28 x 12 months) and request a refund after 2 months,
you are eligible for a refund of approximately $280 (10 months remaining).

EXCEPTIONS (no refunds under any circumstances):
- Accounts suspended for terms of service violations
- Promotional or discounted pricing plans
- Add-on purchases (storage upgrades, extra API quotas)

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND:
Email billing@acmecloud.com with subject "Refund Request - [your account email]" and
include:
- Your account email
- Your reason for requesting a refund
- Your invoice number (found in Settings > Billing)

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.

SECTION 6: CANCELLATION
---

You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan.

When you cancel:
- Your access continues until the end of the current billing period
- No further charges are made
- Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted
- You can re-subscribe at any time to restore your data within the 90-day window

To pause instead of cancel: Business and Enterprise plans support a 1-month pause (once
per year). Contact support to activate a pause.

SECTION 7: ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
---

CHANGING YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS
Go to Settings > Profile > Email. A verification link will be sent to your new email.
Your old email remains active until the new one is verified.

CHANGING YOUR PASSWORD
Go to Settings > Security > Change Password. You will need your current password.
If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA. Supported methods:
- Authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password)
- SMS (available on Starter and above)
- Hardware security keys like YubiKey (Business and Enterprise only)

Enable 2FA at Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication.

DELETING YOUR ACCOUNT
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. To delete your account:
1. Cancel your subscription first (if on a paid plan)
2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
3. Confirm by typing your email address

All data (projects, tasks, files, comments) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Acme Cloud works on all modern browsers:

- Apple Safari 14+

A stable internet connection is required. Acme Cloud does not support full offline mode.

FILE UPLOAD LIMITS
- Free plan: 10 MB per file, 5 GB total

SECTION 10: SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE
---

DATA STORAGE
All data is stored on AWS servers in the US (us-east-1) by default.
Enterprise customers can choose EU (eu-west-1) or Asia-Pacific (ap-southeast-1) regions.

ENCRYPTION
- Data in transit: TLS 1.2 and 1.3
- Data at rest: AES-256 encryption

COMPLIANCE
- SOC 2 Type II certified (Enterprise plan)
- GDPR compliant (data processing agreements available)
- CCPA compliant
- HIPAA compliant (Enterprise plan with Business Associate Agreement)

DATA BACKUP
Automated daily backups with 30-day retention. Backups are stored in geographically
separate data centres.

GDPR / DATA REQUESTS
To request an export of your data, go to Settings > Privacy > Export My Data.
To request deletion under GDPR, email privacy@acmecloud.com.

SECTION 11: COMMON ISSUES AND TROUBLESHOOTING
---

CANNOT LOG IN
1. Check that Caps Lock is off
2. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
3. If using SSO, contact your IT admin
4. If your account was suspended, you will see a specific "Account suspended" message — contact billing@acmecloud.com
5. Clear browser cache and cookies, then try again
6. Try an incognito/private browser window

SLOW PERFORMANCE
1. Check your internet connection speed (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
4. Clear browser cache
5. Check our status page at status.acmecloud.com for any ongoing incidents

CANNOT UPLOAD FILES
- Check that the file is within your plan's size limit
- Supported file types: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, JPG, GIF, MP4, ZIP, and most common formats
- If the upload fails repeatedly, try a different browser
- For files over 100MB, use our desktop upload tool (download at acmecloud.com/desktop)

NOTIFICATIONS NOT WORKING
1. Check your notification preferences at Settings > Notifications
2. For email notifications, check your spam folder and whitelist notifications@acmecloud.com
3. For Slack notifications, ensure the Acme Cloud Slack app is installed in your workspace
4. Mobile push notifications require the app to have notification permission in your device settings

LOST ACCESS TO MY ACCOUNT (2FA ISSUE)
If you cannot access your 2FA device:
1. Use a backup code (generated when you set up 2FA, stored in Settings > Security)
2. If you don't have backup codes, contact support with proof of identity
